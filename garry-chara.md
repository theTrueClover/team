## Notable Projects

### CharaROM

A custom Android ROM that blends technical stability with a very intentional design philosophy: **“Uniquely Chaotic.”**

CharaROM isn’t just another AOSP derivative, it’s:

* A fully functional Android distribution
* A platform for experimentation
* A reflection of personal identity and creative direction

#### Highlights:

* Custom features beyond standard ROM expectations
* Unique branding and identity (not just a reskin)
* Experimental UI elements, including unconventional font integrations
* Clean build pipeline and OTA packaging system

It started as a way to avoid dealing with certain people.
It became something I’m genuinely proud of.

---

### BunnyPad

A Python-based notepad application focused on simplicity and reliability.

Instead of trying to be a full IDE, BunnyPad is designed around:

* Ease of use for non-technical users
* Stability over feature bloat
* Clean, predictable behavior

Ironically, despite its simplicity, it ended up being recognized in places I never expected, like Android modding communities.
